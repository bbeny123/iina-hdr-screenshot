  • None: Disables tone-mapping entirely. Screenshot may be washed-out, overly dark, or clipped
  • Hable: Preserves both dark and bright details, but slightly darkens the overall image
  • Mobius: Prioritizes color accuracy and contrast preservation. May lose some details
  • Reinhard: Preserves overall brightness using nonlinear contrast, but flattens details and degrades colors
  • Clip: Hard-clips out-of-range values. Perfect in-range color accuracy, but distorts highlights
  • Enabled: Provides reference-quality color conversion using zimg. Requires FFmpeg with --enable-libzimg
  • Disabled: Relies on FFmpeg's built-in scale filter. Colors may appear shifted, dark, or desaturated
Screenshots are captured at the nearest keyframe. The resulting image may not be the exact paused frame.
